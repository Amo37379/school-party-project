The problem

Tax Rates are very high

People have the right to keep what they earned and with the amount of tax Labour currently takes this isn’t really fair for the citizens. In current situations kiwis are working hard only for tax to take all their hard earned money from them. The finance minister himself said cutting tax isn’t a good use of resource. This indicates that the money you earn is a good resource for the government before its even taxed.

Also here are the 7 new taxes labour has introduced in just the past 5 years.

  • The Regional Fuel Tax in Auckland.
  • A Light Rail Tax for Aucklanders.
  • The 10-year bright-line test extension.
  • The Tenant Tax removing interest deductibility on rental properties.
  • The new 39 per cent top income tax rate.
  • The Car Tax punishing anyone who needs a truck or ute.
  • The Jobs Tax which targets employees and businesses to subsidise unemployment benefits.

    • Our Plan

    Creating a new tax system

    Unlike Labour, React NZ believes that Kiwis have the right over their own money rather than the government. For this reason we have made reducing tax a priority for the betterment of kiwis and NZ in general.
    We have created a new simple income tax system to ensure that every kiwi has a fair reduction of tax. This new system takes the five tax rates on income down to only three.
    The thresholds would change as follows:

  • 0 - 30,000: 8%
  • 30,001 - 80,000: 15%
  • 15% 80,001 and over: 28%

    • With these tax rates kiwis will have less pressure on paying tax and can have their money in their own hands.
