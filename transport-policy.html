The problem

Public Transport Lacking

Recently transport in some places around NZ has been lacking. With the closure of railway lines in auckland, the annoying and inconvenient cancellations of buses/trains and short staff shows how much of a problem this is. Above all these issues there is also climate change. The amount of greenhouse gases transport produces each year is significantly high. All these factors combined made us think about this problem. At React NZ we believe that public transport should be used more than it is. But to do that we need to manage our systems to provide convenience to the users. This is why we created a APTS (Advanced Public Transport System) plan. This plan would change the system in the country and would reduce carbon emissions by a huge margin.

Our Plan

Advanced Public Transport System (ATPS)

ATPS Will:

  • Recruit more staff
  • Make more convenient timings and less cancellations
  • Advanced plan that will renovate the railway lines without interruptions with the public
  • Cut fares by 25%
